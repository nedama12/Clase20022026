Interpretation

This visualization presents the results of applying the K-Means clustering algorithm to a dataset of 1000 records. Each color represents a distinct cluster, and the highlighted markers correspond to the centroids of each group. The algorithm iteratively assigns data points to the nearest centroid and updates their positions until convergence is achieved. The reduction in variance across iterations demonstrates how the model improves cluster compactness and accuracy.
