Customer Spending Prediction

Machine Learning Regression Model

Problem

Businesses want to predict how much a customer will spend in the future. Understanding customer behavior is essential for optimizing marketing strategies.

Machine Learning Type

Supervised Learning (Regression)

Input Data

Customer age, past purchases, income level, and visit frequency

Expected Output

Estimated spending amount

Real-world Relevance

Companies can personalize marketing strategies, improve customer segmentation, and increase revenue by targeting high-value customers.
