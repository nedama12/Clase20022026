House Price Prediction

Machine Learning Regression Model

Problem

Real estate companies need to estimate the price of a house based on its characteristics. Determining an accurate price can be complex due to multiple influencing factors.

Machine Learning Type

Supervised Learning (Regression)

Input Data

House size, number of rooms, location, and age of the property

Expected Output

Estimated house price

Real-world Relevance

This model helps buyers and sellers make informed decisions, ensures fair pricing, and improves efficiency in the real estate market.
